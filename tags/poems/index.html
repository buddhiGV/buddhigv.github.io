Walls Let’s build walls, A beautiful wall, Just hear my calls, Each and all. For each & every question, If the answer really matters, Then make a donation, No ropes and no ladders. What is your name? What’s your age? Sir or a dame? Warrior or a mage? Left or Right? Where do you stay? Stand-up or oversight? What words do you say? Twitter or Facebook? What arms do you bear?
